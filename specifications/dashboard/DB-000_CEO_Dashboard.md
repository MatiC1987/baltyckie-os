# DB-000 – CEO Dashboard

## Status
Draft

## Business Goal
Provide the owner with a single screen showing the current and future condition of the company to support fast business decisions.

## Priority Widgets
1. Forecast company balance.
2. Current company balance.
3. Forecast vs Actual.
4. Sales pace.
5. Occupancy forecast.
6. Upcoming payment obligations.
7. Critical alerts.
8. AI recommendations.

## Business Rules
- Forecast is displayed before Actual.
- Company-level KPIs take priority over apartment-level metrics.
- Every widget supports drill-down.
- Critical issues are displayed first.
