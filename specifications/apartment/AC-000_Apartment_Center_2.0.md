# AC-000 – Apartment Center 2.0

## Status
Draft

## Business Goal
Apartment Center is the primary operational screen of Bałtyckie OS. It should allow the owner to assess the condition of a single apartment as a business unit and quickly decide whether action is required.

## Key Principles
- Apartment is a business unit.
- Forecast is always shown before Actual.
- Annual summary is more important than monthly apartment result.
- Monthly section focuses on revenue performance.
- Costs are always visible.
- Payment preview links to the full payment module.
- Relationship history links to Partner timeline.

## Main Sections
1. Hero
2. Annual CEO Summary
3. Monthly Revenue Performance
4. Cost Table
5. Payment Preview
6. Relationship Timeline Preview
7. Quick Actions
