# AC-001 – Apartment Card

## Status
Draft (Living Specification)

## Purpose
Define the complete structure and behaviour of the Apartment Card. This is the primary operational workspace for managing a single apartment.

## Design Principles
- Apartment is a business unit.
- Forecast before Actual.
- CEO summary before operational details.
- One-screen decision support.
- Drill-down instead of information overload.

## Main Sections
1. Hero Header
2. CEO Summary
3. Revenue Forecast vs Actual
4. Costs
5. Cash Flow Impact
6. Contracts
7. Payment Preview
8. Documents
9. Relationship Timeline
10. Operational Notes
11. Quick Actions

## Acceptance Criteria
- User understands apartment health within 30 seconds.
- Every KPI supports drill-down.
- No duplicated information.
- Consistent with Business Principles and ADRs.
