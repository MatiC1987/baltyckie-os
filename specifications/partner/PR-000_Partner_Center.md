# PR-000 – Partner Center

## Status
Draft

## Business Goal
Partner Center is the single source of truth for every business relationship with a Partner.

## Scope
- Partner profile
- Apartments
- Contracts and annexes
- Documents
- Payment overview
- Communication history
- KPIs
- Forecast summary

## Functional Requirements
- Display all apartments linked to a Partner.
- Manage contracts and annexes.
- Show payment status and forecasts.
- Store documents and notes.
- Provide complete cooperation timeline.
- Aggregate KPIs across all apartments.

## Business Rules
- One Partner may manage multiple apartments.
- Documents and history are immutable.
- Financial summaries aggregate all active contracts.
- Partner Center links directly to Apartment Center and Payment Center.
