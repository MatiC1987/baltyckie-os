# 000 – Product Vision

**Status:** Approved  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last updated:** 2026-06-28  

## Mission

Bałtyckie OS is the operating system for managing Apartamenty Bałtyckie as a business.

The system is not built primarily to record data. It is built to help the owner understand the current and future condition of the company and make better business decisions.

## Product goal

The owner should be able to answer key business questions quickly:

- Is the company executing the forecast?
- What is the current and future company cash position?
- Which apartments are performing above or below forecast?
- Which costs are out of control?
- Which payments must be made today?
- Which agreements or partner relationships require action?
- Can the company safely add more guaranteed-rent apartments?
- Which decision should be made next?

## Product philosophy

Bałtyckie OS is not a classic PMS, CRM, or ERP.

It is a decision-support system for an apartment rental operator.

Apartments, partners, contracts, reservations, costs, payments, and forecasts are data inputs. The product value is created when those inputs are converted into business insight and clear actions.

## Three mandatory questions

Every feature must help answer at least one of these questions:

1. What happened?
2. Why did it happen?
3. What should I do?

If a feature does not help answer any of these questions, it should not be built.

## Primary users

Bałtyckie OS is built for a very small internal team:

- Mateusz – owner / decision maker
- Mateusz's father – partner / co-owner
- Jolanta Głodkowska – accounting and documentation

The application is not designed for public SaaS distribution at this stage.

## Product direction

The application should evolve from the current e-Bałtyckie 2.0 system into Bałtyckie OS without losing existing business data or operational continuity.

The product must prioritize:

- decision support,
- forecast control,
- payment control,
- portfolio quality,
- automation,
- clarity,
- premium UX.
