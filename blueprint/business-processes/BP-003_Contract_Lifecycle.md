# BP-003 – Contract Lifecycle

## Purpose
Describe the complete lifecycle of a cooperation agreement with a Partner.

## Process
1. Acquire Partner.
2. Negotiate commercial terms.
3. Sign contract.
4. Register Partner.
5. Add apartment(s).
6. Generate contractual obligations.
7. Generate payment schedule.
8. Generate revenue and cost forecasts.
9. Activate operational management.
10. Handle annexes and contract updates.
11. Add or remove apartments if required.
12. End cooperation or renew.
13. Archive documents while preserving full history.

## Business Rules
- One contract may cover multiple apartments.
- Contracts generate future obligations.
- Forecasts are updated after contract changes.
- Annexes modify contracts without losing history.
- Full document history must remain available.
