# BP-006 – Apartment Lifecycle

## Purpose
Describe the complete lifecycle of an apartment within Bałtyckie OS.

## Process
1. Apartment acquisition opportunity.
2. Business profitability analysis.
3. Partner agreement signed.
4. Apartment created in the system.
5. Operational configuration (pricing, calendars, channels).
6. Apartment becomes active for bookings.
7. Continuous operational management.
8. Revenue, costs and KPIs monitored.
9. Renovations or configuration updates when required.
10. Contract renewal or termination.
11. Apartment archived while preserving full business history.

## Business Rules
- Every apartment is a business unit.
- One apartment always has complete historical traceability.
- Contracts, payments and forecasts are linked to the apartment.
- Historical data is never deleted.
