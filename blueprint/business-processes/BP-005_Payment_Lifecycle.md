# BP-005 – Payment Lifecycle

## Purpose
Describe how payment obligations are created, managed and settled.

## Process
1. Contract becomes active.
2. Payment schedule is generated.
3. Payment obligations appear in Payment Center.
4. Upcoming payments are visible on dashboards.
5. User reviews and generates bank transfers.
6. Payments are confirmed.
7. Settlement history is preserved.
8. Forecast and company balance are updated.

## Business Rules
- Payment schedules originate from contracts.
- Payment generation should minimise manual work.
- Generated transfers remain editable before execution.
- All payments are traceable.
- Historical settlements must never be lost.
