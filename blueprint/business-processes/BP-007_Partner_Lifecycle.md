# BP-007 – Partner Lifecycle

## Purpose
Describe the complete lifecycle of cooperation with a Partner.

## Process
1. Lead acquisition.
2. Initial contact.
3. Property and business evaluation.
4. Commercial offer.
5. Negotiation.
6. Contract signing.
7. Partner onboarding.
8. Active cooperation.
9. Contract amendments.
10. Expansion with additional apartments.
11. Renewal or termination.
12. Historical archive retained permanently.

## Business Rules
- One Partner may own multiple apartments.
- One Partner may have multiple contracts.
- Communication history should remain attached to the Partner.
- Financial summaries aggregate all apartments belonging to the Partner.
- Documents are never deleted.
