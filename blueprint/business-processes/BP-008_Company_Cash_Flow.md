# BP-008 – Company Cash Flow

## Purpose
Define how Bałtyckie OS evaluates company liquidity and future cash position.

## Principles
- Company cash flow has higher priority than individual apartment monthly balances.
- Forecasted cash flow is the primary management indicator.
- Actual cash flow validates forecast quality.

## Inputs
- Apartment revenue forecasts.
- Contractual obligations.
- Company operating costs.
- Confirmed payments.
- Planned investments.
- Taxes and financing.

## Outputs
- Current company balance.
- Forecast balance.
- Liquidity warnings.
- Cash deficit prediction.
- AI recommendations.

## Business Rules
- Dashboards prioritise company liquidity.
- Forecast updates after every significant financial event.
- Cash flow is calculated continuously, not only at month end.
