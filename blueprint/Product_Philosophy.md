# Product Philosophy

Bałtyckie OS is not intended to become another generic PMS, CRM or ERP.

Its purpose is to become the operational system of Apartamenty Bałtyckie.

## Every feature should satisfy at least one of the following goals:

1. Improve business decisions.
2. Save employee time.
3. Reduce operational errors.
4. Increase automation.
5. Improve financial visibility.
6. Simplify complex business processes.

## Product Principles

- Simplicity over complexity.
- Business value over technical elegance.
- Consistency over novelty.
- Automation over manual work.
- Long-term maintainability over quick fixes.
- Data should explain what happened and what should happen next.

## Success Criteria

A feature is valuable only if it makes the company faster, safer or more profitable.

Features without measurable business value should not be implemented.
