# Business Glossary

This glossary defines the core business vocabulary used throughout Bałtyckie OS.

## Apartment
The primary business unit. It aggregates revenue, costs, forecasts, contracts, payments, documents and operational history.

## Partner
The entity cooperating with Apartamenty Bałtyckie. A partner may be a private owner, company or another business entity.

## Contract
A legal agreement defining cooperation terms. Contracts create future financial obligations and forecasts.

## Annex
A document modifying an existing contract without replacing it.

## Forecast
The expected future value used for planning and decision making. Forecast is always presented before Actual.

## Actual
The realised value collected from operations.

## Variance
The difference between Forecast and Actual.

## Company Balance
The overall financial condition of the company. It is a more important KPI than the monthly balance of an individual apartment.

## Payment Schedule
The planned list of obligations generated from contracts and business rules.

## Apartment Center
The primary operational module used to manage one apartment as a business unit.

## Partner Center
The module managing the complete relationship with a Partner, including contracts, apartments, documents and payments.
