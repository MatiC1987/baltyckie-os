# 001 – Product Principles

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0

These principles are mandatory for every future implementation.

## PP-001 Forecast First
Forecast is always presented before actual values.

## PP-002 Single Source of Truth
Every business value has exactly one place where it can be edited.

## PP-003 Single Data Entry
The same business information is never entered twice.

## PP-004 Explainability
Every KPI must allow the user to discover where it comes from.

## PP-005 Impact Awareness
Every business change must automatically update all dependent calculations.

## PP-006 Decision Driven UI
Every screen exists to support a business decision.

## PP-007 Progressive Disclosure
Show summary first. Details only after user interaction.

## PP-008 Dedicated Ownership
If a dedicated Center exists (Payments, Costs, Forecasts...), other screens only display a summary and navigation.

## PP-009 Relationship Timeline
History belongs to the business relationship (partner + contract), not only to an apartment.

## PP-010 AI Assists, Business Rules Decide
AI explains, analyses and recommends. Business rules calculate and control the system.

## PP-011 Responsive by Design
Desktop, tablet and mobile share the same business logic but may use different layouts.

## PP-012 Blueprint Before Code
No significant implementation starts before the Blueprint section is approved.

## PP-013 Small Iterations
Every development task must have a unique ID, clear scope and acceptance criteria.

## PP-014 Premium Quality
The application should provide an experience comparable to Apple's first-party business software: clarity, consistency, speed and attention to detail.
