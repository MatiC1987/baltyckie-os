# Product Roadmap

This roadmap defines the recommended order of development for Bałtyckie OS.

## Phase 1 - Foundation
- Documentation
- Repository audit
- Apartment Center
- Partner Center

## Phase 2 - Core Business
- Contract Center
- Forecast Engine
- Payment Engine
- Cost Engine

## Phase 3 - Operations
- Company Dashboard
- Company Balance
- Financial Reports
- Notifications

## Phase 4 - Automation
- AI Assistant
- Automatic payment generation
- Contract generation
- Workflow automation

## Phase 5 - Integrations
- Hotres
- Accounting
- Banking
- Future external integrations

Development priority should always be driven by business value rather than technical convenience.
