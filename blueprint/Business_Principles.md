# Business Principles

This document contains the fundamental business principles that guide every decision in Bałtyckie OS.

## Core Principles

1. Forecast before Actual.
2. Support decisions, not only reporting.
3. Apartment is a business unit.
4. Company financial condition is more important than monthly apartment balance.
5. Every important value has a single source of truth.
6. One screen should answer one business question.
7. Summaries belong on the main screen; details are one click deeper.
8. Contracts create future obligations and forecasts.
9. Automation should reduce manual work whenever possible.
10. AI supports decisions but never replaces business rules.

## Product Philosophy
Bałtyckie OS is an operating system for Apartamenty Bałtyckie, not a generic PMS. Every new feature should improve operational efficiency, decision quality or automation.
