# Research

This directory stores research material that supports product and business decisions.

## Possible topics
- UX inspiration
- Competitor analysis
- Market research
- Technical investigations
- AI experiments
- Integration research
- Financial modelling

## Recommended document structure
1. Objective
2. Sources
3. Findings
4. Conclusions
5. Recommendations
6. Follow-up actions

Research documents are informative only. Product decisions become binding only after being documented in an ADR or Specification.
